About Us

At Buns & Blaze, we're not just serving burgers we're igniting a flavor revolution.

Founded with a passion for flame-grilled perfection and handcrafted quality, Buns & Blaze was born from the idea that every burger should be an experience. Our name reflects our essence: "Buns" that are freshly baked, soft yet bold, and "Blaze" that symbolizes our signature open-flame grilling, where every patty meets fire and flavor in perfect harmony.

We’re proud to be among the first restaurants in New York City to offer 100% halal certified meat a mission we pursued with dedication and purpose. In a city as diverse as NYC, we recognized the need for a burger experience that’s not only bold and unforgettable, but also inclusive and accessible to everyone.

From locally sourced ingredients to innovative recipes that push the boundaries of tradition, our mission is simple to deliver burgers that are memorable, mouthwatering, and made with uncompromising quality.

Whether you're here for a quick bite or a shared moment with friends, Buns & Blaze is where good food meets good fire and everyone is welcome at the table.
