Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Bun & Blaze

Effective Date: April 22, 2025

At Bun & Blaze, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order, make a reservation, or contact us.
  • Payment Information: when you make purchases online (handled securely via third-party payment processors).
  • Usage Data: such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction time through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders, reservations, and customer service requests.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our website.
  • To send occasional promotional emails (only if you've opted in).
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect our business and users.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., delivery platforms, payment processors) who help us operate our business, as long as they comply with strict privacy standards.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, track performance, and remember preferences. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
